## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp green onion, chopped
- 1 tbsp cilantro, chopped
- 1 tbsp red chili, chopped
- 4 tbsp soy sauce

## Steps
1. Mix everything well.

## Tips
1. This is a simple all-purpose dipping sauce for hotpot, meat, or vegetables.
