## Ingredients
- 4 tomatoes, diced and deseeded
- 1/2 white onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, finely diced
- 1 jalapeño, diced
- Small bunch of cilantro, chopped
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 packet chicken bouillon
- 1 tsp ocean salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

## Steps
1. Dice the tomatoes, white onion, garlic, jalapeño, and cilantro.
2. Add everything into a mixing bowl.
3. Add lime juice, olive oil, chicken bouillon, salt, and black pepper.
4. Mix well until everything is evenly combined.

## Tips
1. Best served with tortilla chips, tacos, grilled chicken, or rice bowls.
