## Ingredients
- 50g dried shrimp, soaked
- 40g dried chili, soaked
- 1 whole garlic, peeled into cloves
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp salt
- Some water, as needed
- A large amount of oil, for frying

## Steps
1. Blend the dried shrimp, dried chili, garlic, and a little water until roughly smooth.
2. Heat a large amount of oil in a pan.
3. Add the blended chili mixture and stir-fry over medium heat.
4. Add sugar and salt.
5. Continue stir-frying until the mixture starts to dry out.
6. Add a second batch of oil when the first batch of oil begins to dry out.
7. Keep stir-frying until the chili oil becomes dark brown, dry, and fragrant.
8. Let it cool before storing.
