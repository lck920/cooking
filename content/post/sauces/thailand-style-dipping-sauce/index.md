## Ingredients
- 2 tsp garlic, minced
- 1 tsp red chili, chopped
- Few pieces cilantro
- 1 tsp calamansi or lime juice
- 1 tsp sugar
- 3 tsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 4 tbsp Sprite
