## Ingredients
- 10 cili padi
- 10 green limes, squeezed into juice
- 8 shallots
- 1 whole garlic, peeled into cloves
- 1 bunch coriander, chopped (香菜)
- 5 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp sugar

## Steps
1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Taste and adjust with more lime juice, fish sauce, or sugar if needed.
