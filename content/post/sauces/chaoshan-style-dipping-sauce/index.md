## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp satay sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp garlic, minced
- 2 tsp red chili, chopped
- Few pieces cilantro

## Steps
1. Mix everything well.

## Tips
1. Add a little water if the sauce is too thick.
2. This sauce goes well with beef, pork, or hotpot ingredients.
