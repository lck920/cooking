## Ingredients
- 20 cili padi
- 5 green limes, squeezed into juice
- 50g shrimp paste (belacan)
- 10 shallots
- 2 garlic cloves
- Salt, to taste
- Sugar, to taste
- Some water, as needed

## Steps
1. Sauté the sambal ingredients until fragrant.
2. Blend everything until smooth.
3. Add salt and sugar to taste.
4. Adjust with a little water if the sauce is too thick.
