## Ingredients
- 6 red chilies
- Cili padi, as needed
  - Add more if you prefer it spicier
- 1 small piece ginger
- 3 garlic cloves
- 5 calamansi limes
- Water, as needed

### Seasoning
- Calamansi lime juice
- 1/2 tsp salt
- A little sugar

## Steps
1. Soak the red chilies and cili padi in salted water for 5 minutes.
2. After 5 minutes, drain the chilies.
3. Remove the seeds from the red chilies.
4. Slice the chilies, ginger, and garlic.
5. Add the sliced chilies, ginger, and garlic into a blender.
6. Add a little water and blend until smooth.
7. Pour the blended chili sauce into a bowl.
8. Prepare 5 calamansi limes and squeeze out the juice.
9. Pour the calamansi juice into the chili sauce.
10. Add salt and sugar to taste.
11. Mix well and adjust the taste if needed.
