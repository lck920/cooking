## Ingredients
- 2 red cili, seeds removed and sliced
- 5 shallots, cut in halves
- 5 garlic cloves, cut in halves
- 3 tbsp bean paste
- 200ml water
- 2 tbsp sour plum sauce
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tbsp pepper

## Steps
1. Blend the red cili, shallots, and garlic.
2. Sauté the blended paste until fragrant.
3. Add bean paste and mix well.
4. Add water and bring it to a boil using high heat.
5. Add sour plum sauce and mix well.
6. Add salt, sugar, and pepper.
7. Mix until the sauce is smooth, then serve or use with fish.

## Tips
1. Remove the cili seeds if you want it less spicy.
2. Add more water if you want a thinner sauce.
