---## Ingredients

- 1 bowl cooked white rice
- 1 egg
- 1 spring onion
- White pepper
- Salt
- Cooking oil
## Steps

1. Separate the spring onion into green and white parts.
2. Chop the green part finely and cut the white part into sections.
3. Fry the spring onion white part in oil until fragrant, then remove it.
4. Add egg into the rice and mix well.
5. Add spring onion oil into the pan.
6. Add the egg rice mixture and stir fry until the rice grains separate.
7. Add chopped spring onion, salt, and white pepper.
8. Stir fry evenly and serve.
## Tips

- Use overnight rice for better fried rice texture.
- Mixing egg with rice first helps coat each grain.
- Fry until the rice becomes dry and separate.
