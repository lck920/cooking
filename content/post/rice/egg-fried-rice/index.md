## Ingredients
- 1 bowl cooked rice
- 2 eggs
- 1 stalk scallion / spring onion
- Hot oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- A little water
- Chili oil (Optional)

## Steps
1. Slice the scallion and separate the white stem part from the green leaves.
2. Heat oil in a pan until hot.
3. Separate the egg whites and egg yolks.
4. Slowly pour the egg whites into the hot oil while stirring continuously, so they become light and crispy.
5. Remove and drain the fried egg whites. Keep the egg-flavoured oil for frying the rice.
6. Mix the egg yolks evenly with the cooked rice until every grain is coated.
7. Use the egg oil to sauté the scallion stems until fragrant.
8. Add the egg-yolk-coated rice into the pan and stir-fry until the rice becomes dry, loose, and grainy.
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
10. Add the green scallion leaves and stir-fry briefly.
11. Add a small amount of water to make the rice slightly moist and tender.
12. Serve the fried rice together with the crispy fried egg whites on top.

## Tips
1. When breaking up the rice, press it gently instead of chopping it with the spatula. This helps separate the rice grains better and makes the fried rice more loose and grainy.
2. Avoid smashing the rice too hard, or it may become sticky and mushy.
