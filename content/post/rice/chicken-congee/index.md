## Ingredients
### Congee
- 1 rice cup white rice
- 1,900 ml water
- 14 g ginger, sliced into thin strips

### Chicken Marinade
- 340 g chicken, thinly sliced
- 2 tsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tsp chicken bouillon
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

### Seasoning
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp chicken bouillon

### Garnish
- 2 green onions, chopped
- 1 Cilantro, chopped

## Steps
1. Wash the rice 2–3 times, then drain.
2. Boil 1,900 ml water in a pot.
3. Slice the ginger into thin strips, then chop the green onions and cilantro.
4. Slice the chicken thinly.
5. Marinate the chicken with cornstarch, oyster sauce, water, chicken bouillon, and vegetable oil. Mix well.
6. When the water is boiling, add the washed rice and stir once.
7. Let it boil again, then partially cover the pot.
8. Cook on medium heat for about 25 minutes.
9. Whisk the congee for 2–3 minutes to break down the rice and make it smoother.
10. Turn the heat to high, then slowly add the marinated chicken while stirring.
11. Cook for 1–2 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
12. Add ginger, salt, and chicken bouillon. Stir well.
13. Turn off the heat and serve with green onions and cilantro.

## Tips
- Do not add rice before the water is boiling.
- After adding rice, avoid stirring too much so it does not stick to the pot.
- If the rice is still too firm after 25 minutes, cook for another 10 minutes.
- Add the chicken slowly so it does not clump together.
- Best served hot.
