## Ingredients

### Salsa Sauce

- 1/2 white onion, diced
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1 cili padi, minced
- 1 tbsp garlic, minced
- Cilantro, sliced
- Basil, sliced
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Tabasco
- Olive oil
- Fish sauce, optional

### Fried Rice

- 1 bowl cooked rice
- 1 egg
- Tomato sauce
- 2 tbsp salsa sauce
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Cooking oil

## Steps

1. Dice the white onion, tomato, and cili padi.

2. Mince the garlic, then slice the cilantro and basil.

3. Add onion, tomato, cili padi, garlic, cilantro, and basil into a bowl.

4. Season with salt, black pepper, Tabasco, and olive oil.

5. Optional: add fish sauce if you want a Thai-style sauce. Leave it out if you want a normal salsa sauce.

6. Mix everything together and set aside.

7. Heat oil in a pan.

8. Add egg and rice, then stir fry until the rice grains separate.

9. Season with salt and black pepper.

10. Add tomato sauce in the middle of the pan and stir fry evenly.

11. Add 2 tbsp salsa sauce and stir fry again.

12. Serve the fried rice and top with extra salsa sauce.

## Tips

- Use overnight rice for better fried rice texture.
- Add more Tabasco or cili padi if you want it spicier.
- Add fish sauce only if you want a more Thai-style flavour.
