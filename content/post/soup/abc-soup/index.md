## Ingredients
- 500g pork bones or ribs 
- 1 corn
- 2 tomatoes
- 2 potatoes, ideally Desiree potatoes
- 2 carrots
- 1 white onion
- Water
- Salt and papper, to taste

## Steps
1. Cut the carrots using a roll cut so the pieces are even and bite-sized.
2. Cut the potatoes into large, chunky pieces.
3. Carefully cut the corn into manageable rounds.
4. Cut the tomatoes and white onion into large pieces.
5. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the pork bones or ribs.
6. Skim off the scum that rises to the top.
7. Add the prepared vegetables to the pot with the pork bones.
8. Boil the soup for about 1 to 1.5 hours.
9.  Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

## Flavor Profile
1. White onion provides the base flavour of the soup.
2. Carrots add natural sweetness.
3. Potatoes make the soup more filling.
4. Corn adds a fragrant and earthy note.
5. Tomatoes give subtle acidity to balance the richness of the pork broth.
