## Ingredients
- 1kg pork bones
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3L water
- 2 tbsp lard, or cooking oil
- 15g goji berries
- 200g radish, cut into chunks
- 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 20g ginger, sliced

## Steps
1. Soak the pork bones in cold water with salt for 1 to 2 hours.
2. Rinse the pork bones to remove blood and impurities.
3. Place the pork bones in cold water and bring to a boil.
4. Add ginger slices and Shaoxing wine.
5. Skim off the scum, blood, and foam.
6. Strain the pork bones and rinse them with warm water.
7. Heat lard or oil in a wok.
8. Add the pork bones and stir-fry on high heat until slightly golden and fragrant.
9. Add boiling water into the wok and bring it to a strong boil.
10. Transfer everything into a pot if needed.
11. Add radish chunks and simmer gently for 1.5 to 2 hours.
12. Add goji berries in the last 10 to 15 minutes.
13. Adjust salt to taste and serve hot.

## Tips
1. Use boiling water after stir-frying the bones to help make the broth milky.
2. Do not add goji berries too early because they can affect the broth colour and lose their sweetness.
3. If you want a whiter broth, skip the goji berries.
