## Ingredients
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup whipping cream
- 1/2 white onion, sliced
- 1 tbsp flour
- Mushrooms, sliced
- Unsalted butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Olive oil

## Steps
1. Slice the onion and mushrooms.
2. Heat a pan on low heat and sauté the onions until golden brown.
3. Add the mushrooms and sauté until golden brown and the mushrooms release water.
4. Add flour and stir fry until evenly mixed.
5. Add milk and wait for it to simmer.
6. Pour the mixture into a blender.
7. Add some extra milk while blending until smooth.
8. Pour the blended mixture back into the pot and reheat it.
9. Add unsalted butter, whipping cream, salt, and black pepper.
10. Stir well until the soup becomes creamy and hot.
11. Serve and drizzle with some olive oil on top.
