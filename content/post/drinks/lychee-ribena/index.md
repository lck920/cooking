## Ingredients
- 40 ml Ribena syrup
- 40 ml lychee juice
- 100 ml water
- 3 whole lychees

## Steps
1. Add Ribena syrup, lychee juice, and water into a glass.
2. Stir well.
3. Add ice.
4. Add whole lychees.
5. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. Add more lychee juice if you want a stronger lychee taste.
