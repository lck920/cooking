**Hot Version**
## Ingredients
- 150 ml kopi base
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- Hot water, to fill the cup

## Steps
1. Add kopi base and sugar syrup into a cup.
2. Pour hot water until the cup is full.
3. Stir well.
4. Serve hot.

**Cold Version**
## Ingredients
- 150 ml kopi base
- 10 ml sugar syrup

## Steps
1. Add kopi base and sugar syrup into a glass.
2. Stir well.
3. Add ice.
4. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. For less sweet, use half sugar syrup.
2. For no sweet, skip the sugar syrup.
