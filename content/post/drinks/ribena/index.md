Ingredients:
- 40 ml Ribena syrup
- 140 ml water

Steps:
1. Add Ribena syrup into a glass.
2. Add water and stir well.
3. Add ice.
4. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. Adjust the water amount if you want it lighter or stronger.
