## Hot Version
Ingredients:
- 40-60 ml teh base
- 20 ml sugar syrup
- Hot water, to fill the cup

Steps:
1. Add teh base and sugar syrup into a cup.
2. Pour hot water until the cup is full.
3. Stir well.
4. Serve hot.
## Cold Version
Ingredients:
- 60 ml teh base
- 120 ml water
- 20-30 ml sugar syrup

Steps:
1. Add teh base, water, and sugar syrup into a glass.
2. Stir well.
3. Add ice.
4. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. For less sweet, use half sugar syrup.
2. For no sweet, skip the sugar syrup.
