**Hot Version**
## Ingredients
- 40 g Milo powder
- Hot water, to fill the cup

## Steps
1. Add Milo powder into a cup.
2. Pour hot water until the cup is full.
3. Stir until smooth.
4. Serve hot.

**Cold Version**
## Ingredients
- 50 g Milo powder
- 125 ml hot water

## Steps
1. Mix Milo powder with hot water until smooth.
2. Pour into a glass with ice.
3. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. Milo O has no milk, so the taste is stronger and less creamy.
