## Ingredients

### Kopi Mixture
- 200 ml kopi base
- 200 ml milk mixture
- 20 ml sugar syrup

### Teh Mixture
- 200 ml teh base
- 100 ml milk mixture
- 20 ml sugar syrup

### Cham Mixture
- 225 ml kopi base
- 75 ml teh base
- 300 ml milk mixture
- 30 ml sugar syrup

### Milk Mixture
- 320 g carnation milk
- 80 g ideal milk
- 600 g condensed milk
- 800 g water

## Steps
1. Mix the ingredients for the base you need.
2. Stir until fully combined.
3. Store chilled if not using immediately.

## Tips
1. Stir well before using because the mixture may settle.
2. Adjust sugar syrup based on sweetness preference.
