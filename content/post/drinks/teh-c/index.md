**Hot Version**
Ingredients:
- 100 ml teh base
- 60 ml carnation milk
- 20 ml ideal milk
- 15 ml sugar syrup

Steps:
1. Add all ingredients into a pitcher.
2. Steam the mixture to 65°C.
3. Create a thin layer of foam.
4. Serve hot.

**Cold Version**
Ingredients:
- 120 ml teh base
- 60 ml carnation milk
- 20 ml ideal milk
- 30 ml sugar syrup

Steps:
1. Steam the mixture to 40°C.
2. Create a thin layer of foam.
3. Pour into a glass with ice.

## Tips
1. For less sweet, use half sugar syrup.
2. For no sweet, skip the sugar syrup.
