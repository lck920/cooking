**Hot Version**
Ingredients:
- 100 ml teh base
- 100 ml milk mixture

Steps:
1. Add teh base into a cup.
2. Steam the milk mixture to 65°C.
3. Pour the milk mixture into the cup.
4. Serve hot.

**Cold Version**
Ingredients:
- 100 ml teh base
- 50 ml milk mixture
- 10 ml sugar syrup

Steps:
1. Steam all ingredients to 40°C.
2. Create about 1 cm thick foam.
3. Pour into a glass with ice.

## Tips
1. For less sweet, reduce or skip the sugar syrup.
