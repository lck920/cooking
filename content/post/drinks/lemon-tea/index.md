**Hot Version**
Ingredients:
- 40 ml teh base
- 10 ml lemon juice
- 20 ml sugar syrup
- Hot water, to fill the cup
- Lemon slice, for garnish

Steps:
1. Add teh base, lemon juice, and sugar syrup into a cup.
2. Pour hot water until the cup is full.
3. Stir well.
4. Garnish with lemon slice.
5. Serve hot.

**Cold Version**
Ingredients:
- 60 ml teh base
- 20 ml lemon juice
- 30 ml sugar syrup
- 100 ml water
- Lemon slice, for garnish

Steps:
1. Add teh base, lemon juice, sugar syrup, and water into a glass.
2. Stir well.
3. Add ice.
4. Garnish with lemon slice.
5. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. For less sweet, use half sugar syrup.
2. For no sweet, skip the sugar syrup.
