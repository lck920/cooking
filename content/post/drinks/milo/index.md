## Hot Version
Ingredients:
- 40 g Milo powder
- 20 g condensed milk
- Hot water, to fill the cup

Steps:
1. Add Milo powder and condensed milk into a cup.
2. Pour hot water until the cup is full.
3. Stir until smooth.
4. Serve hot.
## Cold Version
Ingredients:
- 50 g Milo powder
- 25 g condensed milk
- 100 ml hot water

Steps:
1. Mix Milo powder, condensed milk, and hot water until smooth.
2. Pour into a glass with ice.
3. Serve cold.

## Tips
1. For less sweet, use half condensed milk.
