## Ingredients
- Pasta, as needed
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- 3-4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- Olive oil, as needed
- Crushed red pepper, to taste
- Fresh basil leaves
- Pasta water, as needed

## Steps
1. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half.
2. Thinly slice the garlic.
3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-low heat.
4. Add garlic and cook until lightly golden.
5. Add crushed red pepper and stir for a few seconds.
6. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they start to burst.
7. Cook the pasta in boiling water until al dente.
8. Add the cooked linguine into the tomato sauce.
9. Add a little pasta water and toss until the sauce becomes glossy.
10. Add fresh basil leaves and toss again.
11. Serve hot.
