## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2–3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp chilli flakes, chilli padi or dried chilli
- Pasta
- Reserved pasta water
- 1 tsp black pepper
- Parsley, chopped

## Steps
1. Cook the pasta in salted water and reserve some pasta water before draining.
2. Add the olive oil to a pan over low heat.
3. Cook the garlic and chilli gently until the garlic is lightly golden, not burnt.
4. Add a splash of pasta water to stop the garlic cooking and begin forming the sauce.
5. Add the pasta and toss until the oil and pasta water emulsify into a glossy sauce.
6. Add more pasta water as needed.
7. Finish with black pepper and parsley.

## Terms
- Deglaze: add liquid to loosen the flavour from the pan.
- Emulsify: combine oil and pasta water into a silky sauce.
