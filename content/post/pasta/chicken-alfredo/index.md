## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts, cut in half lengthwise
- 45 ml olive oil
- 1/2 tsp chipotle chilli powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp Italian herbs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 450 g pasta
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 3 tbsp minced garlic
- 30 g butter
- 120 ml heavy cream
- 50 g grated parmesan cheese
- Parsley, for garnish

## Steps
1. Season the chicken with olive oil, chilli powder, paprika, Italian herbs, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
2. Cook the chicken in a hot pan for 4–5 minutes on each side until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
3. Cook the pasta until al dente, then drain.
4. In the same pan, cook the onion for 1 minute.
5. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
6. Add butter and let it melt.
7. Add heavy cream and parmesan cheese. Stir until the sauce becomes smooth.
8. Add the cooked pasta and mix well with the sauce.
9. Slice the chicken and place it on top of the pasta.
10. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
