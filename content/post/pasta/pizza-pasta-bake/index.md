## Ingredients
- Cooked pasta
- Tomato pasta sauce (Prego)
- Hotdog, thinly sliced
- Mozzarella cheese
- Diced tomatoes
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Mixed herbs

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C for 15 mins.
2. Add the uncooked pasta, tomato pasta sauce, hotdogs, mozzarella cheese, and diced tomatoes into a casserole dish.
3. Mix everything evenly.
4. Add salt, pepper and mixed herbs to taste
5. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, until the pasta is cooked and the cheese is melted.
6. Serve hot.
