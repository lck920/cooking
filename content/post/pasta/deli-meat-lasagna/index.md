## Ingredients
- 340 g ground beef or chicken
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Italian seasoning, to taste
- 1/2 jar tomato pasta sauce
- 1 egg
- 2-3 tbsp parmesan cheese
- 2-3 tbsp mozzarella cheese
- Sliced bacon or ham

## Steps
1. Cook the ground beef or chicken with salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning.
2. Add tomato pasta sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
3. Mix egg, and parmesan cheese in a bowl.
4. In a baking dish, layer sauce, parmesan mixture, mozzarella cheese, and sliced ham or bacon.
5. Repeat the layers one more time.
6. Top with more sauce and mozzarella cheese.
7. Cover with foil and bake at 190°C for 25 minutes.
8. Remove the foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly.
9. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

## Tips
1. Do not let the foil touch the cheese, or it may stick.
2. Let the lasagna rest before cutting so it holds its shape better.
