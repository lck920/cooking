## Ingredients
- Eggs, as needed
- Butter, as needed

## Steps
1. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over low heat.
2. Add the eggs.
3. Stir constantly while gently scraping the pan.
4. Cook until the eggs are softly set.
5. Smooth the surface of the eggs.
6. Carefully fold and roll the omelette into shape.
7. Serve immediately.

## Tips
1. A French omelette should be soft and lightly set inside.
2. Keep the heat low and avoid browning the outside.
