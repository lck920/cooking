## Ingredients
- 1 egg
- Butter, as needed
- Chilli crisp, optional

## Steps
1. Melt butter in a pan over medium-low heat.
2. Crack the egg into the pan.
3. Cook without flipping.
4. Cover the pan with a lid to steam the egg white.
5. Cook until the white is set but the yolk is still runny.
6. Let the butter brown slightly.
7. Finish with chilli crisp before serving (Optional).

## Tips
1. Covering the pan cooks the top of the egg without flipping.
2. Brown butter adds a nutty flavour.
