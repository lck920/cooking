## Ingredients
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 3 eggs, beaten
- Tomato sauce
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 1/2 tbsp minced ginger
- Cooking oil
- Water

## Steps
1. Dice the tomatoes and beat 3 eggs in a bowl.
2. Heat oil in a pan over high heat.
3. Slowly pour the beaten egg into the hot oil while stirring quickly to make crispy egg bits.
4. Remove the crispy egg bits from the pan and drain the excess oil.
5. Keep some of the egg oil in the pan.
6. Add minced ginger, diced tomato, and minced garlic. Stir fry until fragrant.
7. Add tomato sauce and stir fry evenly.
8. Add some water and continue stir frying until the tomato sauce becomes slightly saucy.
9. Add the crispy egg bits back into the pan and stir fry until coated with the sauce.
10. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. Use high heat when making crispy egg bits so the egg becomes crispy.
2. Pour the egg slowly while stirring fast to create small crispy pieces.
3. Add water little by little so the sauce does not become too watery.
4. Best served with rice.
