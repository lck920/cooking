## Ingredients
- Eggs, as needed
- Cold sliced butter, as needed
- Salt, pepper and chili flakes to taste

## Steps
1. Add eggs into a cold non-stick pan.
2. Add cold sliced butter.
3. Cook slowly while stirring.
4. Keep the pan on the heat for 30 seconds.
5. Remove from the heat for 30 seconds while still stirring.
6. Repeat until soft, silky curds form.
7. Remove from the pan while the eggs are still slightly soft.
8. Add salt, pepper and chili flakes to taste

## Tips
1. Starting with a cold pan helps control the texture.
2. The eggs will continue cooking after being removed from the heat.
