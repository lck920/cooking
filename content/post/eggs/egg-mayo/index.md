## Ingredients

- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- Mayonnaise
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- Black pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Peel the hard-boiled eggs.
2. Cut the eggs in half.
3. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites.
4. Dice the egg whites.
5. Mash the egg yolks and mix with mayonnaise.
6. Add the diced egg whites and mix well.
7. Spread on bread and serve.

## Tips
1. You can chill the egg mayo before serving for a fresher taste.

# Egg Sandwich

## Ingredients

* 1 knob butter
* 1 cheese slice
* 2 eggs
* 2 slices buttered bread
* 1 cili padi, chopped, optional
* 1 pinch salt
* 1 pinch pepper
* Mayonnaise
* Chili sauce, or any sauce you like

## Steps

1. Beat the eggs with salt, pepper, and chopped cili padi.
2. Heat butter in a pan.
3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
4. Place 2 slices of bread in the middle of the egg.
5. Cook until the egg is set, then flip the sandwich.
6. Add cheese, mayonnaise, and chili sauce.
7. Fold the sandwich and serve hot.

## Tips

1. Use low to medium heat so the egg does not burn.
2. Add cili padi only if you want it spicy.
