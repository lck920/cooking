## Ingredients
- Eggs, as needed
- Water, as needed
- Ice water, as needed

## Steps
1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
2. Gently lower the eggs into the boiling water.
3. Cook based on your preferred yolk texture:
   - Soft-boiled: 5 minutes
   - Medium-boiled: 7 minutes
   - Hard-boiled: 10 minutes
4. Transfer the eggs into ice water.
5. Peel and serve.

## Tips
1. 5 minutes gives a runny yolk.
2. 7 minutes gives a jammy yolk.
3. 10 minutes gives a fully set yolk.
