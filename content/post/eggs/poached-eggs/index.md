## Ingredients
- 1 egg
- Water, as needed
- Vinegar, as needed

## Steps
1. Heat water in a pot until gently simmering.
2. Add a little vinegar to the water.
3. Crack the egg into a small bowl.
4. Stir the water to create a gentle whirlpool.
5. Slide the egg into the centre of the whirlpool.
6. Cook until the egg white is set but the yolk is still runny.
7. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain before serving.

## Tips
1. The whirlpool helps the egg white wrap around the yolk.
2. This method is good for Eggs Benedict.
