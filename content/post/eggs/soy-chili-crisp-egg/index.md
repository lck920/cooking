## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp light soy sauce
- 1–2 tsp Lao Gan Ma or chilli crisp
- Spring onion, sliced

## Steps
1. Fry the eggs sunny-side up or make soft scrambled eggs.
2. Mix light soy sauce with Lao Gan Ma or chilli crisp.
3. Add the sauce to the eggs in the pan, or spoon it over the cooked eggs.
4. Add sliced spring onion on top.
5. Serve with rice or toast.

## Tips
1. Use less chilli crisp if you want it less spicy.
2. Cover the pan briefly if you want the spring onion slightly softened.
