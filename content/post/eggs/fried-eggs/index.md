## Ingredients
- 1 egg
- Butter, as needed

## Steps
1. Melt butter in a pan over medium-low heat.
2. Crack the egg into the pan.
3. Cook until the bottom and egg white are mostly set.
4. Flip the egg carefully.
5. Continue cooking based on your preferred yolk texture.

## Tips
1. Over-easy gives a runny yolk.
2. Over-medium gives a partly set yolk.
3. Over-hard gives a fully set yolk.
4. Butter gives the egg a richer flavour.
