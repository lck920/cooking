## Ingredients
- 1-2 heads garlic, peeled
- Olive oil
- 225 g butter, softened
- Parsley or coriander, chopped
- Red chilli flakes
- Salt
- Black pepper
- 1 loaf French bread
- Parmesan cheese
- Shredded mozzarella cheese

## Steps
1. Place garlic cloves in an oven-safe pan and cover with olive oil.
2. Roast at 190°C until the garlic is soft and golden.
3. Blend the roasted garlic with softened butter, parsley or coriander, chilli flakes, salt, and black pepper.
4. Cut the French bread in half lengthwise.
5. Spread the roasted garlic butter generously on both halves.
6. Add parmesan cheese and shredded mozzarella on top.
7. Bake at 175°C until the bread is crispy and the cheese is melted.

## Tips
1. Roast the garlic slowly so it becomes sweet and soft.
2. Do not burn the garlic, or it will taste bitter.
