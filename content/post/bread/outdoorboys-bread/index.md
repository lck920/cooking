## Ingredients
- 400g flour
- A pinch of sugar
- A pinch of salt
- 1 tsp yeast
- Warm water, as needed (start with about 240 ml)

## Steps
1. Put the flour into a bowl and make a well in the middle.
2. Add some warm water to the well, then add the yeast and sugar.
3. Add the salt to the flour away from the yeast.
4. Leave for about 5 minutes, until the yeast mixture becomes bubbly.
5. Add more water gradually while kneading the dough.
6. Knead until the dough is smooth and elastic.
7. Leave to rise for about 1 hour, or refrigerate overnight in a covered container or bag.
8. Heat oil in pan, tear off a piece of dough and cook it in the pan.
9. Serve with honey butter and drizzle with honey (Optional)
