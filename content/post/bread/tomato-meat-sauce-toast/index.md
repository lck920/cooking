---## Ingredients

- 300g minced beef
- 150g minced pork
- 12 onion, diced
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 cup red wine
- 1 can chopped tomatoes
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 3 jalapeños, chopped
- 1 tbsp Italian mixed herbs
- 1 baguette
- 2 tbsp cheese powder
- Basil
- Olive oil
- Water
- Black pepper
## Steps

1. Dice the onion and chop the jalapeños.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion until soft.
3. Add minced beef and minced pork, then stir fry until fragrant.
4. Add minced garlic, chopped tomatoes, and tomato paste.
5. Add red wine and a little water.
6. Simmer until the sauce becomes thicker.
7. Season with Italian herbs, cheese powder, and black pepper.
8. Slice the baguette and toast until crispy.
9. Spoon the tomato meat sauce onto the bread.
10. Top with jalapeños and basil, then serve.
## Tips

- Simmer longer for a richer meat sauce.
- Toast the bread before adding sauce so it stays crispy.
- Add more jalapeños if you want it spicier.
