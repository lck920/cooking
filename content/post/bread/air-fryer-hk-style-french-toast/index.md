## Ingredients
- 4 slices square white bread
- 2 eggs
- 2-3 tbsp kaya jam
- 2 pieces butter
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp sugar
- Condensed milk or maple syrup, for serving

## Steps
1. Cut off the bread crust if you prefer.
2. Spread kaya jam on 2 slices of bread.
3. Place the other 2 slices of bread on top and press down gently.
4. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
5. Dip both sides of the bread into the egg mixture.
6. Place the bread in the air fryer.
7. Air fry at 200°C for 7 minutes.
8. Flip the bread and air fry for another 4 minutes until golden brown.
9. Mix cinnamon and sugar together.
10. Serve with butter on top and drizzle with condensed milk or maple syrup.

## Tips
1. Press the bread firmly so it sticks together.
2. Do not soak the bread for too long, or it may become too soft.
3. You can air fry the leftover crust and coat it with cinnamon sugar.
