## Ingredients
- Choy sum
- Roasted pork
- 2 tbsp minced garlic
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- Salt, to taste
- Oil

## Steps
1. Heat oil in a pan.
2. Add the roasted pork and stir-fry until hot and slightly crispy.
3. Add minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.
4. Add the choy sum stems first and stir-fry briefly.
5. Add the choy sum leaves and mix well.
6. Add dark soy sauce and oyster sauce.
7. Season with a little salt if needed.
8. Stir-fry until the choy sum is cooked and the sauce is evenly mixed.
9. Serve hot.
