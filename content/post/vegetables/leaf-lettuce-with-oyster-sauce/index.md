## Ingredients
- 1 bunch leaf lettuce
- 1–2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2–3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- 2–3 tbsp water
- Optional: a few drops sesame oil

## Steps
1. Wash the leaf lettuce and drain well.
2. Boil water in a pot. Add a little oil and salt.
3. Blanch lettuce for 20–30 seconds, then remove and place on a plate.
4. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped garlic, and fry until fragrant.
5. Add oyster sauce, soy sauce, sugar, and water. Stir until slightly thick.
6. Pour the sauce over the lettuce.
7. Add sesame oil if you like.
