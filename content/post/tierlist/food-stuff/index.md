## Pasta Tier List
- **S Tier**: Rustichella d’Abruzzo, Li Guori 
- **A Tier**: La Molisana, De Cecco
- **B Tier**: Barilla 
- **F Tier**: San Remo, Kimball

## Olive Oils
- Avoid plastic bottles.
- Go for glass and dark bottles.
- Store it in a dark place.
- Best just to use Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- **Good Brands**: Flippo Berio
- **Premium**: Aganto

## Butter
- **S Tier**: Echire
- **A Tier**: Kerrygold
- **B Tier**: President, Elle & Vire
