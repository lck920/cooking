## Ingredients
### Salsa Sauce
- Tomato, diced
- Onion, diced
- Cilantro, chopped
- Jalapeños, diced
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Olive oil

### Yoghurt Sauce
- Greek yoghurt
- Minced garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Cilantro, chopped

### Minced Pork
- Minced pork
- Onion, diced
- Garlic, minced
- Black pepper
- Cumin powder
- Salt
- Shredded cheese
- Cooking oil

### Taco Bowl
- Lettuce, sliced
- Tomato, diced
- Cucumber, diced
- Onion, diced
- Corn kernels
- Cooked minced pork
- Greek yoghurt sauce
- Salsa sauce

## Steps
1. Prepare the salsa sauce by mixing diced tomato, onion, cilantro, and jalapeños in a bowl.
2. Add black pepper, salt, and olive oil. Mix well and set aside.
3. Prepare the yoghurt sauce by mixing Greek yoghurt with minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and cilantro.
4. Heat oil in a pan, then sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
5. Add the minced pork and stir fry until fully cooked.
6. Season with black pepper, cumin powder, and salt.
7. Add shredded cheese and stir fry until the cheese melts into the pork.
8. For the taco bowl, add sliced lettuce into a bowl as the base.
9. Add diced tomato, cucumber, onion, corn kernels, and cooked minced pork.
10. Top with Greek yoghurt sauce and salsa sauce.
11. Mix everything together and serve.

## Tips
- Use Greek yoghurt for a higher-protein sauce.
- Add more jalapeños if you want it spicier.
- You can replace minced pork with minced chicken or beef.
- Serve immediately so the lettuce stays fresh and crunchy.
