## Ingredients

- 2–3 cucumbers  
- 2 scallions / spring onions, chopped
- 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced or minced
- 1.5 tbsp sesame oil
- 3 tbsp light soy sauce
- 3 tbsp honey
- 4 tbsp rice vinegar
- 3 tbsp chilli oil
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds
- Salt

## Steps

1. Wash and dry the cucumbers, then slice thinly or cut into thick diagonal pieces.
2. Sprinkle the cucumbers lightly with sea salt and let them sit for 10–15 minutes.
3. Gently squeeze or press the cucumbers to remove excess water.
4. Add the cucumbers into a bowl or jar.
5. Add scallions, garlic, sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, chilli oil, and sesame seeds.
6. Toss everything until well coated.
7. Serve immediately, or chill in the fridge for a few hours for stronger flavour.

## Tips

- Use Persian cucumbers if you want a crunchier texture.
- Reduce honey to 1.5–2 tbsp if you prefer it less sweet.
- Chill before serving for a deeper marinated flavour.
