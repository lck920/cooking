## Ingredients
- 1 whole sea bass
- 1 piece ginger
- 2 stalks spring onion
- Rice wine
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- Sesame oil
- Coriander
- 1 red chilli
- White pepper
- Salt
- Cooking oil

## Steps
1. Clean the fish well, especially the blood near the backbone.
2. Make a few shallow cuts on the fish.
3. Sprinkle salt on the fish and let it rest for 5 minutes.
4. Wipe the fish dry.
5. Mix soy sauce, sugar, rice wine, sesame oil, and white pepper.
6. Place ginger and spring onion under and on top of the fish.
7. Pour the sauce over the fish.
8. Steam for 5 minutes, then turn off the heat and let it rest covered for 2 minutes.
9. Add coriander, sliced chilli, and spring onion on top.
10. Heat cooking oil with a little sesame oil, then pour it over the fish.
11. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. Salting the fish first helps reduce the fishy smell.
2. Do not over-steam the fish, or it may become dry.
3. Hot oil makes the ginger and spring onion smell better.
