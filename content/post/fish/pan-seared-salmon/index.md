## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets, skin on
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 30 g butter
- 1 tbsp fish seasoning or spice blend
- 1/2 lemon
- Salt

## Steps
1. Pat the salmon dry with paper towels, especially the skin side.
2. Season both sides with salt.
3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat.
4. Add olive oil, then place the salmon skin-side down.
5. Cook without moving it for 2–3 minutes until the skin becomes crispy and releases from the pan.
6. Flip the salmon.
7. Add butter and fish seasoning to the pan.
8. Lower the heat to medium.
9. Spoon the melted butter over the salmon for 2–3 minutes until cooked through.
10. Squeeze lemon juice over the salmon.
11. Serve hot with your favourite side.

## Tips
1. Dry salmon skin is important for crispy skin.
2. Do not move the salmon too early, or the skin may stick.
3. Basting with butter keeps the salmon moist and flavourful.
