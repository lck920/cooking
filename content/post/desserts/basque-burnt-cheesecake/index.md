## Ingredients
- 500 g cream cheese, softened
- 60 g brown sugar (use 30 g for a less-sweet version)
- 3 large eggs or 4 medium eggs
- 250 ml whipping cream
- 15 g plain flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C for 15 minutes. Line an 8-inch cake tin with baking paper.
2. Mix the softened cream cheese until smooth, then add the sugar.
3. Add the eggs one at a time, scraping the bottom and sides of the bowl between additions.
4. Mix in the whipping cream, flour, salt and lemon juice until smooth.
5. Pour into the lined tin and bake at 200°C for 45–50 minutes, until the top is deeply browned and the centre still jiggles slightly.
6. Cool, then refrigerate before serving, ideally for up to 24 hours.

## Notes
- Use half the brown sugar for a less-sweet cheesecake.
