## Ingredients
- 2 egg yolks
- 30 g sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- A pinch of salt
- 100 ml whipping cream
- 100 ml fresh milk
- 2 tsp sugar, for topping
- 2–3 ramekins, about 8 cm wide

## Steps
1. Whisk the egg yolks, sugar, vanilla extract and salt until combined.
2. Heat the milk and whipping cream over low heat until about 70°C; do not boil.
3. Slowly pour the heated milk and cream into the yolks while whisking continuously.
4. Strain the custard through a fine sieve.
5. Pour into ramekins and skim off bubbles or foam on top.
6. Place the ramekins in a hot water bath.
7. Bake at 150°C for 30–35 minutes, until just set with a gentle wobble.
8. Cool at room temperature, then chill for at least 2 hours.
9. Sprinkle sugar on top and caramelise with a blowtorch before serving.

## Terms
- Passing the custard through a sieve is called straining.
