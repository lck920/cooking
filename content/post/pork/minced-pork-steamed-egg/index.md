---## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 100g minced pork
- 3 shallots, sliced
- 1 spring onion, chopped
- 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
- Soy sauce
- Sugar
- Salt
- Water
## Steps

1. Beat 3 eggs in a bowl.
2. Add water using a **1:2 or 1:3 egg-to-water ratio**.
3. Add salt and soy sauce, then mix well.
4. Strain the egg mixture to remove bubbles and unmixed egg.
5. Steam for about 10 minutes.
6. While steaming, sauté shallots and spring onion.
7. Add minced pork and stir fry until fragrant.
8. Add minced garlic and cook until the pork is fully cooked.
9. Season with sugar and soy sauce.
10. Add a little water and simmer briefly.
11. Pour the minced pork topping over the steamed egg and serve.
## Tips

- Cover the bowl with a plate while steaming to prevent water drops.
- Use low to medium heat for smoother steamed egg.
- Add white pepper if you want more aroma.
