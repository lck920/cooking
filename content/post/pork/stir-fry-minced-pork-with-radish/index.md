## Ingredients
- Minced pork
- Sweet radish
- 2 cili padi, chopped
- 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- Salt, to taste
- Oil

## Steps
1. Heat oil in a pan.
2. Add the minced pork and stir-fry until cooked.
3. Add the sweet radish and chopped cili padi.
4. Stir-fry everything together until fragrant.
5. Add dark soy sauce and oyster sauce.
6. Mix well until the minced pork becomes darker and evenly coated with sauce.
7. Taste and add a little salt if needed.
8. Serve hot.
