## Ingredients
- 500g pork belly, sliced into small pieces
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
- 3–5 red or green chillies, sliced
- 2 tbsp shrimp paste
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 1/2 cup coconut cream
- 1/2 cup water
- Black pepper
- Salt, to taste

## Steps
1. Slice the pork belly, onion, ginger, and chillies.
2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
3. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
4. Add the pork belly and stir fry until lightly browned.
5. Add shrimp paste and stir fry until evenly mixed with the pork.
6. Add water and coconut milk.
7. Simmer for 25–30 minutes until the pork becomes tender.
8. Add the chillies and coconut cream.
9. Continue simmering until the sauce becomes thick and creamy.
10. Taste and adjust with salt and black pepper.
11. Serve hot with rice.

## Tips
1. Use pork belly for a richer and more flavourful sauce.
2. Add more chillies if you want it spicier.
3. Do not boil too hard after adding coconut cream, or the sauce may split.
4. Best served with warm white rice.
