## Ingredients
- 1 packet Shin ramen
- 500 ml water
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp Lao Gan Ma
- Protein of choice, optional
- Extra Lao Gan Ma, for garnish

## Steps
1. Slice the spring onions and separate the white and green parts.
2. Add water, ramen seasoning, peanut butter, and Lao Gan Ma into a pot.
3. Bring it to a boil and stir until the peanut butter melts.
4. Add the noodles.
5. Add protein of choice and the white part of the spring onions.
6. When the noodles are almost cooked, slowly pour in the beaten eggs while stirring.
7. Turn off the heat and add half of the green spring onions.
8. Pour the ramen into a bowl.
9. Garnish with the remaining green spring onions and extra Lao Gan Ma.
10. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. Pour the egg in slowly while stirring to thicken the broth.
2. Peanut butter makes the ramen richer and creamier.
3. Add more Lao Gan Ma if you want it spicier.
