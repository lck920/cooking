## Ingredients
- 1 instant ramen packet
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 2 green onions
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
- Sesame seeds, for garnish
- 1 egg, optional

## Steps
1. Mince the garlic and finely slice the white part of the green onions.
2. Add garlic, white green onions, and ramen seasoning into a bowl.
3. Boil the noodles according to the packet instructions, then drain.
4. Heat the oil in a pan until hot. You can also fry an egg in the oil if you want.
5. Pour the hot oil into the seasoning bowl and stir.
6. Add rice wine vinegar and mix well.
7. Add the cooked noodles and toss until coated.
8. Top with green onions and sesame seeds.
9. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. The oil must be hot enough to bring out the garlic and onion aroma.
2. Add the vinegar after the hot oil for a better flavour balance.
