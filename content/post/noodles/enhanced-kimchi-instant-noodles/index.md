## Ingredients
- 1 pack Shin Ramyun
- 2 tbsp kimchi
- 1 pack enoki mushroom
- 2 crab sticks, or any meat you like
- 1 egg

## Steps
1. Boil water in a pot.
2. Add the noodles and seasoning packets.
3. Add kimchi, enoki mushroom, and crab sticks.
4. Cook until the noodles are soft.
5. Crack an egg on top.
6. Let it cook for a short while, then serve hot.

## Tips
1. Do not overcook the noodles so they stay springy.
2. Add the egg near the end if you want a soft yolk.
