## Ingredients

* 4 pieces instant noodles
* 3 eggs
* 30 g prawns, shells removed
* 100 g lean pork, sliced
* 5 garlic cloves, minced
* 250 g choy sum, cut
* 1 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tsp dark soy sauce
* 1 tsp oyster sauce
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 bowl water
* 3 tbsp cooking oil

## Steps

1. Boil the instant noodles for 2-3 minutes.
2. Drain, rinse, and set aside.
3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok.
4. Add the eggs and scramble them.
5. Remove the eggs and set aside.
6. Add 2 tbsp oil into the same wok.
7. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
8. Add the lean pork and fry until cooked.
9. Add the prawns and fry until cooked.
10. Add water, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and salt.
11. Stir well to mix the sauce.
12. Add choy sum and the cooked noodles.
13. Fry until everything is mixed and the water starts to dry up.
14. Add the scrambled eggs back in.
15. Toss everything together and turn off the heat.
16. Serve hot.

## Tips

1. Do not boil the instant noodles too long, or they will become too soft.
2. Rinse the noodles after boiling to stop them from overcooking.
3. Use less water if you prefer drier fried noodles.
