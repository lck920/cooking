## Ingredients
- 1 instant ramen packet
- 1 stock cube
- 500 ml water
- 1 tsp corn starch
- 2 tsp cold water
- 1 egg, beaten
- Green onions, for garnish
- Sesame seeds, for garnish
- Sesame oil, optional

## Steps
1. Add the stock cube to 500 ml boiling water.
2. Mix corn starch with 2 tsp cold water to make a slurry.
3. Slowly pour the slurry into the simmering stock while stirring.
4. Slowly pour in the beaten egg while stirring to create thin egg ribbons.
5. Add the noodles and cook according to the packet instructions.
6. Add the ramen seasoning and mix well.
7. Top with green onions, sesame seeds, and a little sesame oil.
8. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. Pour the egg slowly while stirring to create smooth egg ribbons.
2. Corn starch makes the soup slightly thicker.
