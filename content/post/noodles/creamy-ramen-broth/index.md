## Ingredients
- 1 instant ramen packet
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp mayonnaise
- Green onions, for garnish
- Sesame seeds, for garnish

## Steps
1. Add egg yolk, mayonnaise, and ramen seasoning into a bowl.
2. Mix until it becomes a smooth paste.
3. Boil the noodles according to the packet instructions.
4. Save 4-5 tbsp of the hot noodle water.
5. Add the hot noodle water into the seasoning paste and stir until creamy.
6. Add the cooked noodles into the broth.
7. Top with green onions and sesame seeds.
8. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. Add the hot noodle water slowly so the broth becomes smooth.
2. Use only the egg yolk for a richer and creamier taste.
