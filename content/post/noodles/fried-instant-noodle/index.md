## Ingredients
- 3 packets instant noodles
- 1/2 cabbage, shredded
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 red peppers, seeds removed and sliced
- 1 handful green onions, cut into sections
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 tbsp cooking oil

### Sauce
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tsp salt
- A little water

## Steps
1. Shred the cabbage, onion, carrot, and red peppers.
2. Cut the green onions into sections.
3. Beat the eggs and set aside.
4. Boil the instant noodles for 1-2 minutes until just soft.
5. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.
6. Mix the noodles with 2 tbsp cooking oil to prevent sticking.
7. Heat oil in a pan and fry the eggs.
8. Remove the eggs and set aside.
9. Add more oil to the pan if needed.
10. Add the vegetables and stir-fry until the cabbage becomes soft.
11. Add the noodles and sauce.
12. Stir-fry until the noodles are evenly coated.
13. Add the eggs and green onions.
14. Stir-fry quickly, then serve hot.

## Tips
1. Do not boil the noodles too long, or they will become too soft.
2. Rinsing the noodles with cold water helps keep them chewy.
3. Mixing the noodles with oil helps prevent them from sticking together.
