## Ingredients
- Chicken
- 1/2 ginger, sliced
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 10g goji

## Steps
1. Mix the chicken with ginger, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, pepper, salt, and cornstarch.
2. Add goji and sesame oil, then mix well.
3. Marinate for 15 minutes.
6. Steam for 15 minutes, then serve hot.

## Tips
1. Spread the chicken evenly so it cooks faster.
2. Do not over-steam or the chicken may become dry.
