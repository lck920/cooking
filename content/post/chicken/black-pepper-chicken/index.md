## Ingredients
- Chicken
- 4 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 3 tbsp water
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, sliced

## Steps
1. Marinate the chicken with light soy sauce, salt, pepper, cornstarch, and egg yolk.
2. Let it marinate for 10 minutes.
3. Mix black pepper, oyster sauce, sugar, light soy sauce, and water to make the sauce.
4. Coat the marinated chicken with extra cornstarch.
5. Fry the chicken until cooked, then set aside.
6. Sauté garlic, onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper until fragrant.
7. Add the sauce and mix well.
8. Add the fried chicken back into the pan.
9. Stir-fry until everything is evenly coated, then serve hot.

## Tips
1. Fry the chicken until slightly crispy before mixing with the sauce.
2. Add the bell peppers near the end so they stay crunchy.
