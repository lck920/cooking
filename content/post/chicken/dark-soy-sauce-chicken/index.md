## Ingredients
- Chicken
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp pepper
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 potatoes, large diced
- 1 carrot, large diced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 bowl water
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce

## Steps
1. Marinate the chicken with 1 tbsp light soy sauce, pepper, and cornstarch.
2. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
3. Add the chicken and fry until lightly cooked.
4. Add potatoes and carrots, then stir-fry briefly.
5. Add oyster sauce, 1 tbsp light soy sauce, and dark soy sauce.
6. Mix well until everything is coated.
7. Add 1 bowl of water.
8. Cover and cook for about 20 minutes until the potatoes and carrots are soft.
9. Serve hot.

## Tips
1. Cut the potatoes and carrots into similar sizes so they cook evenly.
2. Add more water if the sauce becomes too dry.
