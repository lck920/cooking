## Ingredients
- 400 g chicken breast, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tbsp all-purpose seasoning powder
- 1-2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
- 3 tbsp cooking oil
- 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
- 2 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1/2 carrot, sliced
- 1 large red chilli, sliced
- 1/2 Holland onion, sliced
  
## Steps

1. Add the sliced chicken into a bowl.
2. Heat 3 tbsp cooking oil in a pan over low heat.
3. Add turmeric powder and stir for 5-8 seconds only.
4. Turn off the heat and let the turmeric oil cool slightly.
5. Pour the turmeric oil and minced garlic over the chicken and mix well.
6. Add cornstarch and mix until the chicken is evenly coated.
7. Heat enough oil in a pan for frying.
8. Add the chicken slowly into the hot oil.
9. Fry for 40-60 seconds without stirring too much, then continue frying until golden and cooked.
10. Remove the chicken and drain it in a strainer.
11. In the same pan, fry the carrot, red chilli, onion, and sliced garlic over high heat for 30-60 seconds.
12. Remove excess oil from the pan.
13. Add the chicken back into the pan and mix with the vegetables.
14. Serve hot with rice.

## Tips
1. Cook the turmeric powder on low heat for only a few seconds so it does not burn.
2. Let the turmeric oil cool slightly before adding it to the chicken.
3. Fry the chicken without stirring too early so it can turn golden.
4. Fry the vegetables on high heat for a short time so they stay crunchy.
