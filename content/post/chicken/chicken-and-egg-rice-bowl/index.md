## Ingredients
- Chicken thigh, cut into bite-sized pieces
- Soy sauce
- Oyster sauce
- Black pepper
- Cornstarch
- Chicken stock or water
- Dark soy sauce
- Honey
- Onion, sliced
- Egg, beaten
- Cooking oil
- Cooked rice

## Steps

1. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce, oyster sauce, black pepper, and cornstarch.
2. Mix chicken stock, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and honey in a bowl.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Pan-fry the chicken for 3-4 minutes, then remove and set aside.
5. In the same pan, cook the onion for 2-3 minutes until soft.
6. Pour in the sauce.
7. Add the chicken back into the pan.
8. Simmer for 4-5 minutes.
9. Slowly pour the beaten egg over the chicken and sauce.
10. Let it cook for about 2 minutes until softly set.
11. Serve over hot rice.

## Tips
1. Cornstarch helps the chicken stay tender.
2. Do not overcook the egg; it should be soft and slightly silky.
3. Serve immediately while the rice is hot.
