## Ingredients
- Chicken tenders or sliced chicken fillet
- 480 ml water
- 1 tsp salt
- Flour
- Cornstarch
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Garlic powder
- Smoked paprika
- Sparkling water or beer
- Canola oil or peanut oil

## Steps
1. Mix 480 ml water with 1 tsp salt.
2. Soak the chicken in the saltwater for 20 minutes.
3. Drain and pat the chicken dry.
4. Mix flour, cornstarch, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika.
5. Take some dry mix and add sparkling water or beer to make a wet batter.
6. Coat the chicken in dry mix.
7. Dip the chicken into the wet batter.
8. Coat again with the dry mix.
9. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and fully cooked.
10. For extra crispiness, rest the chicken briefly and fry it one more time.

## Tips
1. Keep the oil around 175–180°C for easier control.
2. Do not overcrowd the pan, or the chicken will not become crispy.
3. Double frying makes the outside crispier.
