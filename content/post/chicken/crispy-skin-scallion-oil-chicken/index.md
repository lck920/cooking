---## Ingredients

- 2 boneless chicken thighs
- 5–6 spring onions
- 1 tbsp white sesame seeds
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- White pepper
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Salt
- Sugar
## Steps

1. Pat the chicken skin dry with kitchen paper.
2. Place the chicken skin-side down in a cold pan.
3. Turn on high heat and pan fry until the skin becomes crispy.
4. Remove the chicken and cut into strips.
5. Use the chicken oil in the pan to sauté spring onion and garlic.
6. Add a little water and simmer briefly.
7. Season with salt, white pepper, soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar.
8. Pour the sauce over the chicken and serve.
## Tips

- Drying the chicken skin helps it become crispier.
- Start with a cold pan so the chicken fat renders slowly.
- Do not cover the chicken after frying or the skin may turn soft.
