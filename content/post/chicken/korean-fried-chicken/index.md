## Ingredients
- Chicken wings
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp salt
- Full cream milk
- 1 tbsp gochujang sauce
- 1 tbsp tomato sauce
- 1 tbsp chicken stock powder
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 egg yolk
- 4 tbsp cornstarch
- 3 garlic cloves, minced

## Steps
1. Cut small crosses on the chicken wings.
2. Marinate the chicken with black pepper, salt, and full cream milk for 30 minutes.
3. Mix gochujang sauce, tomato sauce, chicken stock powder, water, and honey to make the sauce.
4. After 30 minutes, pour out the milk.
5. Mix the chicken with egg yolk and cornstarch.
6. Coat the chicken with extra cornstarch.
7. Fry the chicken until cooked.
8. Fry again on high heat for 1 minute to make it crispier.
9. Sauté garlic and add the sauce.
10. Add the fried chicken and mix well, then serve hot.

## Tips
1. Double frying makes the chicken crispier.
2. Add more honey if you want the sauce sweeter.
