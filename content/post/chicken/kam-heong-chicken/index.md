## Ingredients
- Chicken
- 1 tbsp cooking wine
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 tbsp cornstarch

### Sauce Ingredients
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 shallots, minced
- 2 tbsp minced lemongrass
- 30g minced dried shrimp
- 2 cili padi, sliced
- Curry leaves
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp ketchup
- Water

## Steps
1. Marinate the chicken with cooking wine, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and curry powder.
2. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
3. Mix the marinated chicken with flour and cornstarch.
4. Deep-fry the chicken until cooked, then set aside.
5. Sauté garlic, shallots, lemongrass, and dried shrimp until fragrant.
6. Add curry leaves and mix well.
7. Add some water and bring it to a boil.
8. Add oyster sauce, light soy sauce, sugar, curry powder, dark soy sauce, and ketchup.
9. Add cili padi and the fried chicken.
10. Mix well until the chicken is coated with sauce, then serve hot.

## Tips
1. Add more water if you want more sauce.
2. Dried shrimp gives strong flavour, so sauté it until fragrant before adding the sauce.
