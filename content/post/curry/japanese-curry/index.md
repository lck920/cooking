## Ingredients
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 carrot, chopped
- 2 potatoes, chopped
- Japanese curry cubes
- 700-800 ml water or stock
- 1 tbsp honey, optional
- 50 ml milk, optional
- Protein of choice, optional

## Steps
1. Heat some oil in a pot.
2. Cook the onion and garlic until fragrant.
3. Add carrot, potatoes, and your protein if using.
4. Pour in water or stock.
5. Simmer until the vegetables are soft.
6. Turn off the heat and add the Japanese curry cubes.
7. Stir until the curry cubes fully melt.
8. Turn the heat back on and simmer until the curry thickens.
9. Add honey if you want it sweeter.
10. Add a little milk if you want it creamier.
11. Serve hot with rice.

## Tips
1. Add the curry cubes after turning off the heat so they melt smoothly.
2. Honey makes the curry sweeter, so skip it if you prefer a stronger curry taste.
3. Milk makes the curry creamier, but add only a little.
